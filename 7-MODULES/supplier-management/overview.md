# Supplier Management — Overview

## What Is Supplier Management?

Supplier Management maintains a complete and verified record of every external party that LEO Fashions works with — whether they provide raw materials or perform processing work on garments.

---

## Two Supplier Categories

### Process Suppliers

Do **processing work** on factory materials.

|Type|Work Done|
|---|---|
|Knitting|Knit yarn into fabric|
|Dyeing|Color yarn or fabric|
|CMT|Cut, make, and trim (stitching)|
|Panel Printing|Print designs on fabric|
|Embroidery|Embroider on garments|
|Washing|Wash processed fabric|

### Other Suppliers

**Provide materials or services** to the factory.

|Type|What They Provide|
|---|---|
|Yarn|Raw yarn|
|Fabric|Ready fabric|
|Production Trim|Labels, buttons, zippers|
|Packing Trim|Boxes, bags, tags|
|Logistics Forward|Shipment handling|
|Transport|Material delivery|

## Supplier Record — Full Details

### Basic Information

|Field|Description|
|---|---|
|Supplier ID|Auto-generated — SUP-000019|
|Supplier Name|Full name|
|Contact Number|Phone|
|Address|Full address|
|GST Number|Tax registration|
|PAN Number|PAN card|
|Supplier Types|Multiple types allowed|
|Payment Terms|Days for payment|
|State|Location state|
|Status|Verified / Pending|

### Bank Details

|Field|Description|
|---|---|
|Bank Name|Supplier's bank|
|Account Number|Account number|
|Account Type|Savings / Current|
|IFSC Code|Branch code|
|Account Holder Name|Name on account|

### Verification

|Field|Description|
|---|---|
|Verified By|Admin who verified|
|Verified On|Date of verification|

## Supplier Bills Tab

All invoices from a supplier are tracked here:

|Field|Description|
|---|---|
|Bill Number|SUPBILL-0001|
|Description|What the bill is for|
|Quantity|Qty covered|
|Amount|₹ amount|
|Bill Date|Date raised|
|Bill Photo|Uploaded scan|

**Summary shown:**

- Total Bills count
- Total Quantity
- Total Amount ₹

---

## Key Rules

- Only **verified** suppliers can be used in PO and DC
- Admin is the only one who can verify a supplier
- A supplier can have **multiple types** (e.g. Yarn + Knitting)
- Supplier ID auto-increments: SUP-000015, SUP-000016...
